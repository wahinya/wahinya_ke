About the Candidate

Born Here. Live Here.
Fighting for Here.

I am Wahinya Duncan Waweru — an engineer, a son of Dagoretti South, and someone who is tired of watching our constituency's potential be stolen by corruption and bad policies.

I grew up in these streets. I built my career here. Every day I see brilliant young people with no jobs, roads unfixed for years, and leaders who only show up at election time.

As an engineer, I solve problems — not talk about them. Public money should build classrooms, roads, and opportunities. Not disappear into someone's pocket.

This is not about politics. It is about our children's future. And I need you standing with me.

Engineer delivering real projects on time and on budget

Zero tolerance for corruption — every shilling must be accounted for

Committed to youth jobs, bursaries, and putting Dagoretti South on the map

A leader present every day — not just at election time

The Vision

Five Pillars of Transformation

A comprehensive agenda to create real, lasting change for every family in Dagoretti South.

Education

Bursaries, school infrastructure, and digital literacy programmes for every child in Dagoretti South.

Economic Empowerment

Youth and women enterprise funds, market access, and job creation initiatives.

Healthcare

Upgraded health facilities, affordable medical access, and mental health support for all residents.

Infrastructure

Roads, drainage, clean water, and sanitation for a dignified quality of life.

Good Governance

Transparent use of public funds, open participation, and zero tolerance for corruption.
